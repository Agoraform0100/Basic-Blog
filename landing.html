Ah, The Glory of Meat

Meat is the cornerstone of any great meal, here are some examples:

Steak is truly one of the greatest of meats. It is juicy, flavorful, and rich, but can drastically range in quality depending on the cut, the preparation, and the source of the animal.

Pork Loin melts in the mouth, provided it is properly prepared, fresh, and served with a slice of lime.

Lamb is quite delicious as well, often consisting of more tender meat that is best served after marinating for 24 hours prior to cooking. A quality lamb dish is often served in strips with mushrooms, cream of spinach, and a fine white wine.
